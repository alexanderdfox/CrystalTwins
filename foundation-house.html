Foundation House

A free people built a great House upon one strong Foundation Stone called the Constitution, then added so many rooms of laws over the centuries that the House became vast and bewildering. No person knows all the rooms, yet every room claims its authority by tracing back, however distantly, to that single Foundation Stone.

This game maps the full United States Code (all 54 titles) plus landmark statutes, CFR rules, and executive actions—each with real citations and links to the U.S. Code, Constitution, CFR, and Congress.gov. Green halls are documented steps toward the Constitution.

You begin in a distant regulation or statute. Click only neighboring halls that lie one step closer to the Constitution. Read the citations as you climb back.
